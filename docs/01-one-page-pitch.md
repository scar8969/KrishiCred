# One-Page Pitch: KrishiCred

**Company:** KrishiCred
**Tagline:** Pollution into Profit
**Category:** Climate Tech / Agri-Tech / Carbon Markets

---

## One-Sentence

An AI platform that detects crop residue burning via satellite, routes stubble to biogas plants through WhatsApp, and monetizes farmers' climate-positive behavior through carbon credits.

---

## The Problem

| Metric | Scale |
|--------|-------|
| Stubble burned | 20M tons/year in Punjab |
| Pollution contribution | Up to 44% of Delhi's winter air pollution |
| Health cost | $30B annually |
| Farmers affected | 2.5M+ households |

**Root Cause:** Farmers burn because alternatives cost MORE money than burning.
